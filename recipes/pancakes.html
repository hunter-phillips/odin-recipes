Blueberry Flax Pancakes Recipe

Description

These delicious, healthy pancakes contain blueberries for a perfect taste and ground flax seeds for fiber.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups of pancake mix
  • 0.5 cups of flax seeds
  • 1 cup of skim milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of blueblerries

Steps

  1. Place a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Stir the pancake mix and flax seeds together in a medium-sized bowl.
  3. Whisk together the milk and eggs in a separate bowl.
  4. Mix the wet ingredients into the pancake and flax seed bowl.
  5. Pour a fourth of a cup of batter onto the skillet, and sprinkle the pancake with blueberries.
  6. Flip the pancake once bubbles appear, and remove when browned on both sides.

Scrambled Eggs

Description

These thick, moist eggs are cheesy and easily customizable to please everyone!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of cheedar cheese
  • 1 tsp of heavy whipping cream
  • 0.5 tsp of butter
  • Any additional toppings, such as ham, bacon, or peppers

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Whisk eggs in a bowl.
  3. Mix cheese, heavy cream, and any additional toppings into eggs.
  4. Pour egg mixture into skillet.
  5. Stir for 3 to 5 minutes.
  6. Remove when cooked but still moist.

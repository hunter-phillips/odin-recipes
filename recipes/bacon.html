Crispy Microwaved Bacon

Description

When time is short or an oven isn't an option, cooking bacon in a microwave is a great solution! The bacon tastes just as crispy and delicious as any oven-cooked bacon.

Ingredients

  • Four slices of uncooked bacon

Steps

  1. Place the bacon on a paper-towel-lined plate.
  2. Cover the plate with additional paper towels.
  3. Each microwave's power varies, so it is best to microwave on high and check after 3 minutes.
  4. Depending on how crispy the bacon needs to be, add an minute and repeat until satisfied.
